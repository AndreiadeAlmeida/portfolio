Who Am I?

Hi, my name is Andreia and I’m a Junior Front-End Developer.
I officially started my coding journey at Career Foundry a year ago, and in doing so I found a field I’m incredibly interested in.
After finishing my course I’ve had the opportunity to join a start-up as a junior Front-End developer. Under the supervision of a Senior Developer, I have built responsive web-based applications, from design to coding, for their internal website and mobile App, using React, JavaScript JS6 and SASS.
My years of combined experience as a data analyst, freelancer and as a Junior Front-End Developer have taught me how to work under pressure and to tight deadlines, handling data with precision and adaptability. I’m capable of taking on projects beyond the scope of a web developer due to my experience design skills developed during my degree in A/V and Multimedia, and my VFX qualification from Escape Studios.
